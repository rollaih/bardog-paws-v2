THE PAWS INITIATIVE

Wine for Humans,
Love for Dogs.

The PAWS initiative is at the heart of this campaign. Bar Dog has long been a champion for rescue dogs, and this experience brings that mission to the forefront. Through the interactive Bar Dog agent, users learn about PAWS Chicago, are encouraged to donate, and can purchase Bar Dog wines that support the cause.

  • PAWS campaign featured prominently in the Link In Bio experience
  • Bar Dog guides users to learn about adoptable dogs
  • Direct purchase-to-donate flow within the conversation
  • Campaign content drives traffic to PAWS Chicago

Branded partnership with PAWS Chicago — driving awareness, adoption, and donations.
